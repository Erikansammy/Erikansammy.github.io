// My Story

From Classroom to
Cutting-Edge AI

My journey started in front of a chalkboard, teaching Mathematics and Physics to hundreds of students across primary and secondary schools in Nigeria. I learned early that the hardest skill isn't knowing the answer — it's making complexity simple.

That same mindset carried me into telecoms, where I spent years as a Project Supervisor and NOC Engineer at Uppercrest and Tyllium — managing Huawei infrastructure, hitting 99.5% network uptime, and delivering Power BI dashboards that drove real decisions.

Then AI changed everything. I joined Awarri Technologies as a Machine Learning & Data Annotation Specialist — applying RLHF, labeling multi-modal datasets, and contributing to models that align with human values. Today I combine that technical depth with practical AI automation to help businesses work smarter.
